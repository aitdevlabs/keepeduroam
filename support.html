💬 Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I store time?

Connect to Wi-Fi, open the app, and tap CONNECT. Your time starts storing automatically.

❓ How do I use stored time?

When offline, open the app and tap CONNECT. Your stored time will be used automatically.

❓ How do I extend my time?

Go to the "Earn Time" tab and tap "Watch Ad". Each ad gives you extra time.

❓ Why can't I see any providers?

No one is currently storing time. Try again later or store your own time to help others.

❓ Is my data private?

Yes! We only store time (hours). We never see what you browse or any personal information.

❓ How many ads can I watch?

You can watch 5 ads per day. Each ad gives you 1-4 hours of time.

❓ How much time can I store?

You can store up to 48 hours at one time. Time expires after 30 days of inactivity.

❓ Is KeepEduroam free?

Yes! 100% free. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases. Just optional rewarded ads.

❓ How does time work?

1 hour connected = 1 hour stored. When you use time, it decreases. Watch ads to add more.

❓ What happens if I disconnect?

Your time is saved. You can reconnect anytime. Time only decreases when you're actively using it.
