💬 Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I save a session?

Connect to Wi-Fi, open the app, and tap CONNECT. Your session saves automatically.

❓ How do I use a saved session?

When offline, open the app and tap CONNECT. Your saved session will be used automatically.

❓ How do I extend my session?

Go to the "Earn Time" tab and tap "Watch Ad". Each ad extends your session.

❓ Why can't I see any sessions available?

No one is currently saving sessions. Try again later or save your own session to help others.

❓ Is my data private?

Yes! We only track session time (hours). We never see what you browse or any personal information.

❓ How many ads can I watch?

You can watch 5 ads per day. Each ad gives you 1-4 hours of session time.

❓ How much session time can I save?

You can save up to 48 hours at one time. Sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity.

❓ Is KeepEduroam free?

Yes! 100% free. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases. Just optional rewarded ads.

❓ How does session time work?

1 hour connected = 1 hour saved. When you use a session, time decreases. Watch ads to add more.

❓ What happens if I disconnect?

Your session is saved. You can reconnect anytime. Session time only decreases when you're actively using it.

❓ Can I use KeepEduroam anywhere?

KeepEduroam works best on university campuses with eduroam coverage. Session sharing requires other users nearby.
