KeepEduroam is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We believe in privacy by design.

1. What Data We Collect

📱 Device ID

A unique, anonymous identifier generated on your device. We never collect your name, email, or phone number.

⏰ Session Data

Hours of session time you save and use. We only store the number of hours, never what you do with your session.

🎬 Ad Interactions

When you watch an ad, we record the reward type (6h, 12h, 24h) to extend your session. We do not track which specific ads you see.

📊 App Performance

Anonymous crash reports and usage data to help us improve the app. This never includes your personal information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Track your session balance — So you know how much session time you have saved.
  • Grant session extensions — When you watch an ad, we add time to your session.
  • Improve the app — Crash reports help us fix bugs and improve performance.
  • Show relevant ads — AdMob serves ads based on your device, not your personal data.

3. Data Storage

  • Securely stored — Your data is stored in a secure, encrypted database.
  • Auto-deletion — Your session expires after 30 days of inactivity. We don't keep data forever.
  • No sharing — We never sell or share your data with third parties (except AdMob for ads).

4. AdMob & Google

KeepEduroam uses Google AdMob to serve rewarded ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID (for ad targeting)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interactions (views, clicks)

You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting: Google Ad Settings

5. Your Rights

  • Access — You can see your saved session time at any time in the app.
  • Delete — Change your device ID in Settings to permanently delete all your data.
  • Opt out — You can stop using the app anytime. Your session will expire after 30 days.
  • GDPR/CCPA compliance — We respect your privacy rights.
